Getting Started

New to Connexa? Learn the basics of setting up your account and projects.

How do I create my first project?

Go to the Projects page, click on the + New Project button, and fill in your project details such as name, client, budget, and deadline. Once done, press Create Project.

How do I invite a client?

From your project dashboard, navigate to the Clients tab and click Add Client. You can then enter their email address or import from your contacts.

Account & Settings

Manage your personal information, security preferences, and notifications.

How can I change my password?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Data and click on Change Password. You’ll receive a confirmation email with instructions.

Can I turn off notifications?

Yes! In Settings → Notifications, toggle off the types of alerts you no longer wish to receive.

Projects & Collaboration

Learn how to work efficiently with teams and clients.

Can multiple users edit a project?

Yes, project collaborators can view and edit project details if given permission. You can manage access in the project’s Team tab.

How do I share files securely?

Each project includes a Files section where you can upload, view, and share documents with version tracking and access control.
